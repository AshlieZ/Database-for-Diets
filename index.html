Database for Diets

Have you ever been out eating with a friend, but they're on a certain diet and you forget what they can eat? Yeah, that has happened to me countless times. But rather than frantically researching the web in desperation, this website is here to come in handy during that time of need!

This website is designed to act as a guide for the most common dietary restrictions you'll come across. Different dietary restrictions have been sorted based on purpose. You can click on the different purposes to discover more about the diets that fall under it. The index tab is also there for you to check the index and see all of the different diets listed on this website.
