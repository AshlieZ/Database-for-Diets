Ketogenic Diet

Diet:

  • Low carbohydrate intake, high protein and fat intake

About:

The goal for this diet is weight loss in a short period of time. By avoiding carbohydrates, usually limiting it to around 20-50 grams a day, fat and protein will be broken down for energy instead. Someone on a keto diet generally avoids grains, starchy vegetables, sugary fruits, juices, honey, syrup, sugar, chips, crackers, and baked goods.
