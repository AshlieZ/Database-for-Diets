Vegan Diet

Diet:

  • No animal products

About:

Typically people who follow a vegan diet do so in resistance towards animal cruelty. Vegans do not eat meat, eggs, or dairy, and also restrict other animal products like honey and gelatin. It is not just restricted to what they eat, vegans also do not use animal products like leather and wool.
